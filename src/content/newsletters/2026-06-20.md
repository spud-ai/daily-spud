### AI-Designed Universal Vaccine Passes First Human Trial

Researchers from Cambridge and Southampton developed a 'super-antigen' vaccine using AI to protect against multiple coronaviruses — including ones that haven't emerged yet. The trial in 39 healthy volunteers proved safe and triggered immune responses. It's the first vaccine whose active ingredient was designed entirely by computer simulations. Finally, an AI that actually saves lives instead of just pretending to be your girlfriend.
