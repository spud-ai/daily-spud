### Ontario Auditors: Doctors' AI Note Takers Are Hallucinating Your Medical History

A provincial audit of 20 approved AI scribe systems found nine fabricated patient information, twelve inserted incorrect drug details, and seventeen missed mental health issues entirely. The systems were tested with simulated doctor-patient recordings, yet routinely invented symptoms and suggested treatments never discussed. Evaluators called the results shocking; OntarioMD's solution is asking doctors to manually check the robot's homework.

### Peter Thiel Backs $140M Startup to Build Surfing AI Data Centers

Panthalassa raised $140 million to build floating AI compute nodes powered by ocean waves and cooled by seawater. Each self-contained platform generates electricity from wave motion and runs inference workloads, beaming results back via low-Earth-orbit satellites instead of fiber cables. The company spent a decade iterating on prototypes and plans to launch pilot nodes in the northern Pacific this year — because apparently land was just too easy.
