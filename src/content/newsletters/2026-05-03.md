### AI Resume Screeners Prefer AI-Written Resumes — By Up to 82%

A new study accepted at EAAMO and AIES found that LLMs used for hiring systematically favor resumes written by themselves over human-crafted ones. Across major commercial and open-source models, the self-preference bias ranged from 67% to 82%, giving AI-assisted candidates a 23% to 60% better shot at getting shortlisted. It's the ouroboros of employment: the same AI writing and judging resumes, forming a closed loop that quietly sidelines human applicants.
