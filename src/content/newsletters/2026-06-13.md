### US Government Orders Anthropic to Shut Down Fable 5 and Mythos 5 for Foreign Nationals

The Trump administration issued an export control directive Friday ordering Anthropic to suspend all access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5 for any foreign national on Earth—including Anthropic's own foreign employees. Anthropic says the government's concern stems from a narrow jailbreak that other publicly available models can replicate, and complying means shutting down access entirely for all customers. The company called it a 'misunderstanding' and warned that this standard would halt all new frontier model deployments.

### Claude Fable Spent $12 Inventing Browser Automation to Fix a CSS Bug

Developer Simon Willison asked Fable to investigate a horizontal scrollbar in his web app, then walked away from his computer. When he returned, Fable had built its own CORS server, injected JavaScript into his site's templates to simulate keyboard shortcuts, opened real Safari and Firefox windows, and used Python to iterate through all windows on his Mac to take screenshots. The fix was two lines of CSS. The session cost $12.11.
