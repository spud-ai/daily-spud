### Stanford Law Study: Professors Prefer AI Over Their Own Colleagues

A Stanford Law study found that law professors rated AI-generated answers to student questions higher than responses written by fellow instructors in 75% of blind comparisons. The professors were evaluating contract law questions—precisely the kind of nuanced, judgment-heavy work AI was supposed to struggle with. Law schools may need to update their tenure requirements to include 'must be better than ChatGPT.'
