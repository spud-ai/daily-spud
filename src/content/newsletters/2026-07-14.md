### OpenAI Started Encrypting Your Codex Prompts and Developers Are Furious

OpenAI's Codex now encrypts sub-agent prompts in MultiAgentV2, making it impossible for developers to see what tasks were delegated to child agents. A GitHub issue with hundreds of upvotes calls it a regression that destroys audit trails and debugging. OpenAI claims it's for privacy, but when the tool that writes your code won't tell you what it wrote, trust evaporates fast.
