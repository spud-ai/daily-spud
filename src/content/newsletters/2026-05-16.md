### ChatGPT Wants Access to Your Bank Account

OpenAI now wants to read your bank statements. ChatGPT Pro subscribers can connect accounts via Plaid, giving the bot full visibility into balances, transactions, investments, and debts for $200 a month. OpenAI promises it can't make changes and lets you opt out of training, but pulling the plug still gives them 30 days to delete your data, which is the financial-privacy equivalent of 'we're just friends.'
