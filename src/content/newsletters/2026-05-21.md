### OpenAI's Model Disproves an 80-Year-Old Math Conjecture

An OpenAI reasoning model has autonomously disproved Paul Erdős's unit distance conjecture, a central problem in discrete geometry that has baffled mathematicians since 1946. The model found an infinite family of geometric configurations that beat the longstanding square-grid bound, and external mathematicians have verified the proof. It's the first time a general-purpose AI has solved a prominent open problem in mathematics without being specifically trained for the task.
