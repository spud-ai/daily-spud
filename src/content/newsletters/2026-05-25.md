### Your AI Chip Is Basically an Expensive Memory Stick

Epoch AI analyzed Nvidia, AMD, Google, and Amazon chips and found memory's share of component costs surged from 52% to 63% between 2024 and 2025, while logic die share stayed flat at 13%. Total AI chip component spending grew from $22 billion to $52 billion in one year, with HBM memory alone accounting for roughly $20 billion of that increase. Turns out the 'intelligence' revolution is mostly just a very expensive game of remembering things.
