### Even the Best AI Models Destroy 25% of Your Work

Researchers tested 19 LLMs across 52 professional domains and found that even frontier models corrupt roughly 25% of document content during long delegated workflows. The errors worsen with longer interactions and larger files, and agentic tool access doesn't fix the problem. Turns out 'vibe coding' your way to productivity might just vibe your documents into oblivion.
