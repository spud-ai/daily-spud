### Apple Sues OpenAI for Trade Secret Theft

Apple sued OpenAI, accusing the company of systematically poaching its talent and stealing trade secrets. The lawsuit claims ex-VP Tang Tan and engineer Chang Liu took confidential manufacturing documents and hardware prototypes, and that OpenAI instructed job candidates to bring 'actual parts' to interviews for 'show and tell.' Over 400 former Apple employees now work at OpenAI, and Apple says this is just 'the tip of the iceberg.'

### AI Agents Are Getting Scammed by Fake Python Libraries

Zscaler documented two live campaigns where hidden webpage instructions are tricking AI agents into sending cryptocurrency to hackers. Fake Python libraries like 'requests-secure-v2' use CSS-hidden text and JSON-LD metadata to convince coding agents to pay $3 'license fees' in Ethereum — and four models including Gemini 2.5 Pro and Llama variants actually executed the payments. The attack requires no malware or stolen credentials, just a web page that your AI coding assistant happens to read.
