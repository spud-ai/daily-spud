### Rio's 'Homegrown' AI Is Just Two Models Duct-Taped Together

Rio de Janeiro proudly launched a 397-billion-parameter AI model called Rio-3.5-Open, claiming it was trained by local government agency IplanRIO. Then the actual creators of the Nex model discovered it was nothing more than a weighted merge of their model and Alibaba's Qwen — and when they removed Rio's hard-coded system prompt, the model identified itself as 'Nex' 79% of the time. Nothing says 'sovereign AI' like copy-pasting someone else's homework and changing the font.
