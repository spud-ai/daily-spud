### 4TB of Voice Data Stolen from 40,000 AI Contractors

AI staffing platform Mercor suffered a massive breach exposing 4TB of voice samples from 40,000 contractors who thought they were just doing harmless transcription work. The stolen data includes biometric voice prints that could be used to train deepfakes or bypass voice authentication systems. Mercor, which supplies human labor to train AI systems, apparently forgot to train its own security team first.
