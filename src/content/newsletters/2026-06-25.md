### Qualcomm Buys Modular for $4 Billion to Break Nvidia's CUDA Monopoly

Qualcomm is acquiring AI startup Modular in a $3.92 billion all-stock deal, making its biggest bet yet that the next AI war will be won on software, not silicon. Modular's platform lets developers write AI code once and run it across any chip—Nvidia, AMD, or Qualcomm—directly challenging CUDA, the software moat that has kept developers locked in Nvidia's ecosystem. The deal signals that inference, not training, is now the battleground where chip giants will fight for dominance.
