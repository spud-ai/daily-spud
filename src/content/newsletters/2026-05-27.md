### An Italian Region Just Slapped a 200% Tax on Datacenters in Green Areas

Lombardy, the region around Milan, became the first in Italy to pass a law discouraging datacenter construction with massive fee hikes — 100% more in rural areas and 200% more in parks. With 33 active datacenters in Milan alone and half of Italy's €22 billion in AI infrastructure investment landing on its soil, Lombardy has had enough. It's the first time a government has looked at AI's power hunger and said 'we're going to need a bigger toll booth.'
