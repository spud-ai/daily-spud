### AI Just Designed a Vaccine That Could Stop the Next Pandemic

Researchers at the University of Cambridge have developed a world-first vaccine whose key component was designed entirely by artificial intelligence and is already being trialled in humans. The AI designed a 'super-antigen' that trains the immune system to recognize entire families of coronaviruses, including mutated strains and animal viruses that could jump to humans. The team is already developing similar vaccines for flu and Ebola. For once, the AI is trying to save us instead of replace us.
