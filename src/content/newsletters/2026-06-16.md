### Qualcomm Wants Tenstorrent for $10 Billion Just to Take a Shot at Nvidia

Qualcomm is in talks to acquire AI chip startup Tenstorrent for up to $10 billion, a massive jump from its $3.2 billion valuation months ago. Led by legendary architect Jim Keller, Tenstorrent builds RISC-V AI accelerators challenging Nvidia's dominance. The deal would give Qualcomm serious AI silicon muscle and signal that chip giants would rather buy their way into the race than out-engineer Nvidia from scratch.
