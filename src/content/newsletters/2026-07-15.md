### Australia Just Declared AI Scraping Creative Work 'Theft'

Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese unveiled a world-first national AI framework and a new "Office of AI" inside his own department, declaring that AI companies scraping creative work without permission is outright "theft." The framework promises the "strongest possible protection" for writers, artists, and musicians while fast-tracking domestic data center construction. It's the rare policy move that simultaneously infuriates Silicon Valley and delights Australian creatives — a political two-for-one.
